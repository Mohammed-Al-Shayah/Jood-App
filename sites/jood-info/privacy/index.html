Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (such as name, email, phone number, and profile details) and data generated by your use of the app (such as offers viewed, preferences, and basic usage analytics).

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide offers and improve app performance.
  • To communicate with you regarding support and important updates.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share it with service providers that help us operate the app (such as hosting, analytics, and messaging), under strict confidentiality obligations.

Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion at any time.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system can be 100% secure.

Your Rights

  • Access and update your profile details in the app.
  • Request account deletion from Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  • Contact support if you have questions or requests.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@joodapp.io.
