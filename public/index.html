Delete Jood - Food Offers Account

You can request the full deletion of your account and all associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  • In the app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  • Or send a request to: support@joodapp.com

What Is Deleted?

  • Account data (name, email, or phone number)
  • Profile data
  • Orders and favorites
  • Any data linked to the account

Data That May Be Retained

We may retain legally required records for no more than 30 days for security or accounting purposes, after which they are permanently deleted.

Processing Time

Your deletion request will be completed within 7 business days of receipt.
